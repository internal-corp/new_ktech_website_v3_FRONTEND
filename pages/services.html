EMC Certification

Non-RF/Telecom products require to be certified in many countries priorly before being exported. As this certification is a MUST, KTECH can gladly assist you get the necessary approval of your products.

RF Type Approval

Type approval, or homologation is required in order to import your electronic products into almost every country in the world. Whether it is Bluetooth, WiFi, PSTN, Satellite, Cellular, etc. most regulatory authorities will require either in country testing or submission of foreign test reports along with other required documents. KTECH helps you navigate the various requirements of each country ending in a successful approval of your products. Contact us for details

Energy Efficiency

As more countries around the world become concerned about energy use and the growth of their high tech sector the number of countries that now require or at least suggest you obtain some type of energy efficient certification is growing. In many countries we can utilize your existing test reports but some countries will require samples be sent for in country testing at their specified labs. KTECH assists not only with the Energy Efficiency Certificate applications but coordinates the testing with the in country labs.
